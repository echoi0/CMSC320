Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: The main difference between merging and rebasing is that merging combines the two branches while preserving all of the history (essentially
being the safe option), while rebasing rewrites the previous file to fit all of the new info, which could potentially be a lot more dangerous.
